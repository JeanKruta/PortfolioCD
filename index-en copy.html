Who am I?

My name is Jean Kruta, I am 27 years old, I have a degree in Business Administration, and I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I am transitioning to a career in Data Science and am seeking internship or junior-level positions to apply my knowledge and grow in the profession. I have intermediate proficiency in essential tools such as Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn, scipy, scikit-learn, etc), SQL (MySQL), and Power BI, and I am constantly improving my skills. I am passionate about uncovering insights from data and applying analytical solutions to solve real-world problems.
